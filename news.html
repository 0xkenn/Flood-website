November 5, 1991

a massive flash flood hit Leyte's Ormoc City. Approximately 8,000 people across Ormoc perished in the flood. Isla Verde, a densely populated region close to the city center, had severe flash flooding because of Typhoon Uring (also known as Thelma globally). Such an occurrence that resulted in a landslide and the inability of the area to contain the water due to its capacity being exceeded was caused by illegal logging and deforestation.

September 1998

rivers near Pasig, Marikina, and Taguig overflowed as a result of persistent rain. 707,737 people, or 144,78 families, were impacted by this disaster. There has been flooding in 970 barangays in 104 municipalit ies and 16 provinces. According to medi a reports, a total of 18,000 hectares of rice fields have been destroyed by the floodwaters.

June 2008

Typhoon "Frank" hit the Western Visayas; it generated severe rain that continued for days, flooding the rivers. For most residents in Western Visayas, the floodwater level is the highest they have ever encountered. Nearly 400,000 families were severely impacted , and approximately 500 individuals perished because of the disaster.
